Managing Student Mental Health Awareness Campaign: A Project Management Approach

About the Project

This exhibition showcases my major project, where I apply project management techniques to organize and manage a mental health awareness campaign for students. The project focuses on planning, scheduling, resource allocation, and risk management to ensure the success of the initiative.

Project Overview

  • Project Title: Managing Student Mental Health Awareness Campaign: A Project Management Approach
  • Key Methodologies: Project Planning, Scheduling, Risk Management, Resource Allocation
  • Objective: To raise awareness about mental health issues among students through effective project management strategies.

Project Applications

This project has practical applications in educational institutions, where project management techniques can be used to effectively run mental health awareness campaigns. By using these techniques, institutions can ensure that campaigns are well-organized, within budget, and achieve their intended impact.

Project Impact

By implementing project management principles, this campaign aims to deliver timely mental health awareness initiatives, ensuring the active involvement of all stakeholders, and creating a lasting impact on the student body.
